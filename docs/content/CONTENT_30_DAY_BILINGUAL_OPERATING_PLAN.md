## 2. Hard operating rules

1. Every date below is a **proposed editorial slot**, not a scheduled post.
2. No asset generation, adaptation, scheduling, or publishing occurs from this plan.
3. Post 3 may proceed to owner-controlled asset production only through its already approved Visual Brief and a separate authorization.
4. All other posts are `DRAFT_FOR_OWNER_REVIEW` until copy, media brief, source, and claims pass Quality review.
5. No Batch A1 asset is marked reusable without an exact filename/hash and human visual approval.
6. Use only approved public wording. Do not use free/complimentary claims, prices, years of experience, credentials, invented testimonials, medical/therapy/rehabilitation claims, Adaptive Aquatics claims, or guaranteed outcomes.
7. No children, clients, identifiable learners, or sensitive cases in media without documented permission and separate approval.
8. Coach Ayman imagery must use an approved original source with identity preservation; no face swap or regenerated face/body/clothing.
9. UTM values are placeholders only. They must not be stored or activated before the Analytics/Consent implementation gate.
10. A post is not `Published` until a platform-specific receipt and public verification exist.

## 3. Editorial mix and proposed cadence

The 30-day mix is intentionally balanced:

- Educational: 10
- Trust: 5
- Service explanation: 5
- Engagement: 5
- Conversion: 3
- Local intent: 2

Formats are proposed, not authorized:

- Feed carousel/static: 13
- Reel: 7
- Story-only interaction: 8
- Feed + Story adaptation: 2

The plan avoids daily hard-sell content. Conversion CTAs appear only where the content naturally supports an initial-assessment request.
