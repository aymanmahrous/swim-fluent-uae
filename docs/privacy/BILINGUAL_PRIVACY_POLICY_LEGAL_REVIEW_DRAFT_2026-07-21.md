## 1. Who we are

This policy explains how Relax Fix UAE / Swim Fluent UAE handles personal data when a person uses the website, requests a swimming or water-confidence assessment or coaching, or contacts us. The final controller identity and trade-licence details must be confirmed before publication.

Proposed privacy contact:

## 2. Data we may collect

We collect the minimum information needed to handle a coaching request, which may include:

- Adult requester's name and phone number.
- Selected participant category and gender.
- Requested training location.
- Previous swimming experience and fear-of-water selection.
- Coaching type and requested date/time.
- Request status and creation/update records.
- Evidence that displayed terms were accepted at submission.

We do not request medical reports, diagnoses, medication names, health documents, or free-text medical narratives through the public website or assistant.

## 3. Children's data

An authorized adult submits every request concerning a participant under 18. The current name field represents the adult requester, not the child.

Where data relates to a child under 13, clear parental or guardian consent must be obtained, withdrawal must be easy, and the collection purpose and use must be explained. Children's data is not used for targeted advertising, behavioural profiling, or unrelated commercial purposes.

## 4. Purposes

We use data as needed to:

- Review an assessment or coaching request.
- Contact the requester about level, location, and scheduling.
- Administer the request and prevent duplicates or abuse.
- Protect systems and investigate failures or security incidents.
- Meet legal obligations or respond to competent authorities where applicable.

Submitting a request does not confirm a booking or appointment.

## 5. Legal basis and consent

Processing may rely on consent where required, steps needed to respond to a service request, legitimate operational and security interests within legal limits, or a legal obligation. UAE-qualified legal review must confirm the appropriate basis for each purpose.

## 6. Sharing and providers

Hosting, database, communications, and operational providers may process information only as needed. No provider, country, or log-retention claim should appear publicly until live account settings and contracts are confirmed.

Users may open external services such as WhatsApp, email, or Google Maps, which operate under their own terms.

## 7. International transfers

Some infrastructure or communications services may process information outside the UAE. Relevant recipients, countries, and legal safeguards must be confirmed before publication.

## 8. Retention

We keep information only as long as needed, using initial operational review periods subject to legal approval:

- Inactive, cancelled, or declined requests: review for deletion or anonymisation within 90 days unless required for a legal matter, dispute, or security investigation.
- Active customer records: minimum necessary information with annual review.
- WhatsApp, phone, and email communications: review after 12 months and delete when no longer needed.
- Consent and preference-change records: an initial period of up to 24 months.

These are operational decisions, not final legal or backup-retention statements.

## 9. Security and access

Access is limited to people who need booking data to provide the service or administer systems. Fear-of-water and participant-category values are restricted operational information. Reasonable safeguards are applied, but no electronic method is risk-free.

## 10. Rights

Depending on applicable law, rights may include:

- Information about data types, purposes, recipients, and retention standards.
- A copy or transfer where applicable.
- Correction of inaccurate data.
- Erasure or restriction, subject to legal conditions.
- Objection to or stopping processing, particularly direct marketing.
- Withdrawal of consent for future processing where relied upon.
- A complaint to the competent authority.

Withdrawal does not automatically erase data collected before withdrawal. Deletion is handled as a separate request.

## 11. Privacy requests

Requests may be sent to `relaxfix2026@gmail.com`. Reasonable information may be requested to verify identity or authority as a guardian or representative. No fixed response time should be published until the procedure is approved.

## 12. Analytics and advertising

This draft does not authorize Analytics, GTM, cookies, or attribution storage. Future measurement must be consent-gated, offer clear reject and withdrawal controls, and exclude raw booking data and identifiers. Children's data must not be used for targeted advertising.

## 13. Changes

The policy may be updated when services, systems, or legal requirements change. The published version will show an effective date, and renewed consent will be sought where legally required.
